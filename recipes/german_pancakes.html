German Pancakes

Description

Super good and fluffy german pancakes, lots of butter, so delicous.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 6 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preaheat oven to 350 F
  2. Melt butter in pan
  3. Mix flour, milk, eggs, and salt. Pour into pan
  4. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden brown
  5. *Don't open oven during cooking
